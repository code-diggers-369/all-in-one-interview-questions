Online Auction Overview

Online Auction Process

Maintain your profile, give your requirement or request customised new suppliers for specific product categories. Invite new suppliers as well as your existing suppliers into a competitive process, create and manage your own online eRFx projects using our eTender platform. After Registration you can manage your entire wing-to-wing sourcing process from one online dashboard.

My Profile

Complete and maintain your own personal profile including your Personal details, Contact details and the details of your Company. Here you can also subscribe or unsubscribe to our monthly newsletter. As per our Privacy Policy we never sell, rent, publish or distribute any of your identifiable details to any third party for marketing purposes.

View Messages

As in any Sourcing project Buyers may contact individual Suppliers or issue a message to all Suppliers within their project. Buyers are able to view and manage all their own messages both to and from Suppliers from this single location.

View Request for Supplier

Require a Quick Scan or a Full Scan sourcing project requesting Supplier Scan to source and locate new suppliers on your behalf? Here Buyers complete a Request Form, purchase, manage and view all your projects at any time throughout the project from the beginning up to downloading your Final Report in PDF format.

Packages

Purchase the volume Package to suit your specific requirements for the use of our eTender tool to write your own RFx and directly invite suppliers to participate in your online project. Here you can purchase a single Project or obtain percentage discounts for purchasing multiple project packages.

My Account

The access point for our eTender tool where Buyers create and manage all projects and directly invite suppliers to participate. Each Project includes 3 rounds in any combination of auction; RFP or RFQ! Check the status, view the results or export to your own pc for further analysis as well as downloading your project Final Report in PDF format.

Working Details

This is an additional profile section for all Suppliers to provide further quantitive detail for their company as well as a qualitative "About Us" free text to promote the capabilities of their company. Details here include Quality and Environmental Accreditaions, Health & Safety and Information Security Accreditaions and the company size based on employee numbers.

View Your Product

Add your entire portfolio of product or service categories using our product search engine to locate the correct categories. Our product classification system is the CPV Classification as approved by the EU Commission. Buyers will often search for new suppliers based on a product category, therefore it is essential that Suppliers add all their product categories to their profile.

My News

Upload and manage all your latest news and press releases in the industry standard format of Headline, Summary and Body Text as well as the ability to upload and add a related picture or photo to enhance your news. One free press-release with every profile or limited free press-releases with every higher membership package.
